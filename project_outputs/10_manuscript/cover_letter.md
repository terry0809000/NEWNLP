# Cover Letter

Dear Editors,

We submit "A CPU-feasible benchmark for calibrated biomedical abstract sentence classification with external validation" for consideration as a Research Article in PLOS ONE. This manuscript reports a reproducible CPU-feasible healthcare NLP benchmark using public biomedical abstract datasets. The work evaluates classical and recurrent neural models, reports discrimination and calibration, and includes external validation on a label-compatible PubMed RCT corpus.

The study is intended as a transparent methodological benchmark rather than a clinical deployment claim. All locally generated artifacts, including code, predictions, metrics, tables, figures, and LaTeX source, are organized for reproducibility.
